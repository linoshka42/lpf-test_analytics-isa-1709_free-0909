⚠️ Important: Day trading is high risk. Most retail day traders lose money. The tips below are educational — not financial advice. Never trade more than you can afford to lose, and never day trade within your ISA as losses cannot be offset against gains.
Key Day Trading Concepts
📈
Momentum Trading
Buy stocks that are trending strongly upward with high volume. Look for stocks up 5%+ in pre-market. Use a stop-loss at 2% below your entry. Exit when momentum stalls. Best for: volatile US stocks (NVDA, TSLA, MSTR).
📉
Mean Reversion
Buy when a quality stock drops sharply on no news (5–10% intraday). The price tends to bounce back. Set a tight stop-loss. Best for: large-cap quality stocks like MSFT, AAPL, GOOGL that you already know well.
📰
News Catalyst Trading
Trade around earnings, FDA approvals, M&A announcements. Check the Market News tab for catalysts. Watch: Pfizer, AstraZeneca for trial results. Apple for product launches. Enter pre-announcement, exit on the news.
🛑
Risk Rules — Never Break These
1% Rule — never risk more than 1% of your trading capital on a single trade
Stop-loss always — set it before you enter, never move it down
No ISA — day trade in a separate general account, not your ISA
3 losses = stop — if you lose 3 trades in a row, stop for the day
